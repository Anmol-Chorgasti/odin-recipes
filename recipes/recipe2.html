Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

Peanut Butter Sandwiches are convenient snacks that provide a fresh boost of energy. They are generally eaten during breakfast, or as a preworkout dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices brown bread
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp jam/jelly
  • 2 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and wait until the butter melts
  2. place the bread slices on the butter and let the bread soak up the butter. DO this for both the sides of all the slices until the bread has a dark brown grilled look to it
  3. Apply peanut butter on one slice, and jelly on the other
  4. Press the slices together. (You can also add sliced banana between the two slices of bread for a sweeter taste)
  5. Enjoy!
